OUR STORY

About Levvi's Furniture

Workshop / Craftsmanship Photo

Founded in Mandevu, Lusaka, Levvi's Furniture has been crafting quality, handmade furniture for the local community — built piece by piece, by hand, with care and skill.

MISSION

To provide quality, affordable, handmade furniture to every Zambian home.

VISION

To be Lusaka's most trusted name in furniture and interior design.

VALUES

Craftsmanship, honesty, and customer satisfaction — in every piece we build.

Why Handmade in Lusaka

Every piece we build is handmade right here in Lusaka — not mass-produced. That means real craftsmanship, close attention to detail, and furniture built to last for generations, not seasons.

Our Team

A dedicated team of skilled craftsmen and designers committed to bringing your vision to life, one piece at a time.
